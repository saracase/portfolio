Poster Design

This poster was from an assignment at school. The assignment was based on practising illustration in Illustrator. I did my poster based on Fashion Week in London because I'm a big fan of Fashion Week and my favourite city is London. I chose pink and neutrals for the poster because I wanted it to have a soft look and because I had flowers in my illustration I wanted it to have a Spring feel to it. I used a serif font to fit in with the feel of Fashion Week, very classy.
